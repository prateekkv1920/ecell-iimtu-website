About E-Cell

Building Tomorrow's Entrepreneurs Today

E-Cell IIMT University is a dynamic platform dedicated to nurturing the entrepreneurial spirit and fostering innovation among students. Our mission is to cultivate a culture of creativity, leadership, and business acumen.

We provide resources, mentorship, and opportunities for students to develop their ideas into successful ventures through workshops, hackathons, startup support, and networking events.
