Frequently Asked Questions

Who can join E-Cell IIMTU? ▼
Any student currently enrolled at IIMT University can apply to join E-Cell. We welcome students from all branches and years.
Do I need a startup idea to join? ▼
Not at all! You just need the passion to learn. E-Cell is for everyone — whether you want to start a company someday or just learn about entrepreneurship.
Is there a membership fee? ▼
E-Cell IIMTU is free to join. Some events may have nominal registration fees to cover logistics, but membership itself is completely free.
What will I gain from joining? ▼
You gain access to workshops, hackathons, mentorship sessions, networking events, and a like-minded community of student entrepreneurs. Plus certificates and prizes!
